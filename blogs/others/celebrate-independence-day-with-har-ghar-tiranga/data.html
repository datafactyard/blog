The Har Ghar Tiranga campaign, initiated by the Government of India, has taken the nation by storm, bringing a sense of patriotism and unity to every household. This remarkable campaign encourages citizens to proudly display the national flag, the tricolor, at their homes and workplaces. Not only does it promote a strong sense of national identity, but it also fosters a deep respect for the sacrifices made by our freedom fighters. In this article, we'll dive into the details of the Har Ghar Tiranga campaign, its significance, and how you can obtain your certificate to participate by uploading a selfie with "tiranga" in this remarkable movement.

Har Ghar Tiranga is a campaign launched by the Government of India to encourage people to hoist the national flag in their homes. The campaign is being organized on the occasion of the 75th Independence Day celebrations.

People who participate in the Har Ghar Tiranga campaign will be given a certificate. This certificate will have the person's name, address, and the date on which the national flag was hoisted.

To participate in the Har Ghar Tiranga campaign, people can take a photo after hoisting the national flag in their homes. 

How to Get Har Ghar Tiranga Certificate step by step:

1. Visit the official website of the Har Ghar Tiranga campaign at https://harghartiranga.com/.

2. Click on the "Upload selfie with flag" button.

3. Allow the website to access media.

4. Enter your name.

5. Upload a photo of yourself with the tiranga.

6. Check the consent box.

7. Click Submit.

Once you have successfully submitted it, you will receive a certificate within a few seconds.You can download or share the certificate on social media with hashtag #harghartiranga #azadikamahotsav

You can go to https://harghartiranga.com/selfi to find your uploaded picture.

You can digitally host the tiranga by clicking -https://harghartiranga.com/digital-tiranga
